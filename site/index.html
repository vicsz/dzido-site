Dzido

Dzido, born Zdzislaw Szoltysek, is a skiing enthusiast and philosopher with a passion for humor. He's also the host of the "I Think, Therefore I Google" podcast, where he explores life's peculiarities and engages in light-hearted existential conversations. Join Dzido on his journey through the slopes of both comedy and philosophy.
